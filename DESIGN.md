# DESIGN

## Visual Direction
Committed colour strategy with steel-blue and warm-cyan accents over tinted-light neutrals. The mood is technical daylight, more workshop than startup billboard.

## Colour Tokens (OKLCH)
- Background: oklch(0.97 0.006 235)
- Surface: oklch(0.99 0.004 235)
- Foreground: oklch(0.22 0.02 240)
- Muted Text: oklch(0.50 0.02 240)
- Primary: oklch(0.55 0.16 235)
- Primary Deep: oklch(0.42 0.12 235)
- Accent: oklch(0.70 0.11 195)
- Border: oklch(0.88 0.01 235)

## Typography
- Display and body use Geist Sans for now, with stronger hierarchy through size/weight contrast.
- Body line length target: 65-75ch.

## Layout
- Avoid repetitive equal-card marketing grids as the primary structure.
- Use asymmetric sections with varied vertical rhythm.
- Keep project summaries scannable and link-forward.

## Motion
- Subtle fade/translate reveals only where meaningful.
- Use ease-out timing; no bounce effects.

## Component Notes
- Primary buttons are filled with strong contrast.
- Secondary actions use outlined treatments.
- Project entries should support status and contribution affordances.
